INCEPTION :

  • The state of farmers in India who comprise about 20% of the total population of 1.36 Billion is Poverty-Stricken. The Average Monthly Income of a farmer in Maharashtra is just Rs 8,938. Agriculture is the main source of income for more than 50% of the total population in India.

  • The National Crime Records Bureau of India in its 2017 Report stated that about 12000 Farmers commit Suicide Every Year, this amounts to 11.2% of the total people who committed suicide in India. In the 2013 Maharashtra Drought at least 30 Farmers killed themselves every day, on average. More than 3500 Farmers Committed suicide in 2013 itself. This is very saddening.

  • The Main Reason for this was a Failure to Grow Crops caused by:
    1. Unforeseen climatic conditions
    2. Droughts
    3. Bad agricultural practices
    4. Lack of guidance
    5. Excessive use of pesticides
    6. Low yields and
    7. Unregulated prices
We Aim at alleviating all these problems and provide Free of Cost Services so all Farmers in India can practice Sustainable Agriculture.
